About Me

Troy is an up and coming Full Stack Developer currently completing the Northwestern University Full Stack Developer Coding Bootcamp. Troy is an excellent communicator, organized hard worker, determined team leader and loyal teammate. With his Bachelor’s Degree in Chemical Engineering from Purdue University and 3+ years in the physical engineering industry (Oil & Natural Gas, Power, Cement, and Pollution Control), Troy’s technical dexterity and critical thinking skills are well accomplished. Managing projects ranging in value from $1k-1M+ and deadlines from 1 week to 1+ years, Troy’s scope realization and focus are adept and dynamic.

Technology is the future, with projects large and small. Troy’s project managing and technical problem solving skills will directly translate in his transition into the technology industry.
